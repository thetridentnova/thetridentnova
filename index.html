Explore India's Rich Heritage

Taj Mahal

The epitome of love, this iconic marble mausoleum in Agra is a UNESCO World Heritage site.

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4.9/5)

Khajuraho Temples

Famous for their stunning sculptures and intricate carvings, these temples celebrate Indian art and culture.

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (4.5/5)

Qutub Minar

A remarkable example of Indo-Islamic architecture, this tower is one of the most visited monuments in Delhi.

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4.8/5)

Ajanta Caves

Renowned for their ancient Buddhist frescoes and sculptures, these caves are a marvel of art and history.

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (4.6/5)

Hampi

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, Hampi showcases the grandeur of the Vijayanagara Empire through its ruins and architecture.

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4.9/5)

Sun Temple, Konark

Known for its architectural brilliance, the Sun Temple is designed as a colossal chariot dedicated to the Sun God.

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (4.7/5)
